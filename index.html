Shijie Song, PhD
Assistant Professor

Shijie Song (宋士杰) is an assistant professor at the Business School of Hohai University. His research focuses on information systems, human-computer interaction, and health informatics. Shiijie obtained a Ph.D. degree in information studies from Nanjing University and dual master's degrees in international business and marketing from Groningen University and Newcastle University, respectively.
